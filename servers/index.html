Void Incournic Servers

The Void Incournic servers are hosted with QuadIX. We welcome anyone to join our servers and expect them to follow the rules.

Our two servers we have are Starbound and StarMade. Starbound is a 2D space sandbox game by a company in England called Chucklefish (http://www.chucklefish.org/).

StarMade is a 3D space sandbox game by a company in German called Schine (https://www.star-made.org/).

Void Incournic Starbound Server [Modded]

This is the Starbound server for the community of Void Incournic We are happy to have anyone join us.

Join our discord Server to get the IP address.

NOTE: We do use mods and the Modpack is updated every so often

Mod Pack: https://files.voidinc.net/games/starbound/modpacks/

Void Incournic Rust Server

A rust server for people who do not like their builds to be wiped every month or week. And who want to have a good community to join.

Features:
 - Active Admins
 - No Wiping Monthly or Weekly (no stance on yearly yet)
 - Oxide Plugins:
   - Auto Lanterns
   - VPN Block
   - Third Person
   - Backpacks
   - Skip Night Vote
   - Fancy Drop
   - Sign Artist

   - Others may be added later

Rules:
1. Respect other players. (e.g. harassment, flaming, trolling, etc.)
2. Respect admins, and their decisions, we have the right to make what ever decision and will stand by it. Do not ask for admin at all.
3. Do not hack or cheat, this will result in a permanent ban.
4. Do not advertise your own server or anyone else's for any game.
