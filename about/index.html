About Void Incournic

Void Incournic is an organization made by gamers and programmers. Some of us have a background in art too. We were founded in 2015 when Nick "thakyZ" Mullally, had gathered some friends and created this organization.

We don't only do programming; we also host servers. Our servers we host are related to the games we make mods and content for. We are very open to new ideas, and will accept any mods to be ported for any game we own.

Our games are in development right now, but we are working on them. And our own mods are very basic right now.
